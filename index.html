A Story of Care: Our Journey

A Story of Care:
Our Journey

Pee Safe's story started with a road trip that left our Co-Founder, Srijana Bagaria, with a UTI in a hospital bed. Following her experience, she was determined to find a solution to unhazardous and unhygienic toilet experiences. Her research alongside our Founder, Vikas Bagaria, resulted in developing an impactful solution - The Toilet Seat Sanitiser.

In their journey for care, Pee Safe has built an undeniable place in the hearts and bags of women all around the country. Today, Pee Safe has extended to personal care, menstrual hygiene and female grooming.
